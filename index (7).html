About FundBridge

Financing East Africa's Future

Founded in Kampala, Uganda in 2019, FundBridge was built with one goal: make professional loan management software accessible to every lending business in East Africa — from small microfinance groups to large institutions.

🏦

Our Mission

To democratize access to professional lending software across East Africa, enabling every lender to operate efficiently, transparently, and profitably.

🌍

East Africa Focus

We are built for the realities of doing business in Uganda, Kenya, Tanzania, and Rwanda — with UGX/KES/TZS support, Mobile Money integration, and local compliance.
